About Lions

Lions (Panthera leo) are large carnivorous mammals found in Africa and parts of Asia. Known for their majestic appearance, lions are often referred to as the "King of the Jungle," though they typically inhabit grasslands and savannas rather than dense jungles. Diet

Lions are carnivores, and their diet consists mainly of large herbivores like zebras, antelopes, and buffalo. Lions are also known to scavenge and steal kills from other predators like hyenas.
